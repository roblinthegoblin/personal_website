Expertise

I am a Techno-Economic Analyst working within offshore renewable energy. My day to day job involves assessing the economic viability of different renewable energy technologies that are deployed in the ocean, namely:

  • Offshore wind (fixed and floating)
  • Tidal stream
  • Wave energy

I have over eight years of experience working in this sector. I specialise in the following areas:

Techno-economic assessment

While I come from a scientific background, my career has been shaped by my great interest in economics and finance. Since 2014 I have specialised in techno-economic assessment of offshore renewable energy. This involves designing models and tools to calculate costs and energy yield.

My skill set covers analysis methods such as levelised cost of energy, discounted cash flow, cost and yield assessment and technology appraisal. I frequently combine my love for programming and GIS (more details on these below) with this techno-economic expertise to develop bespoke models in Python and Microsoft Excel. These have been used to advise multinational, world leading companies on their renewable energy investment strategies, contributing towards a greener tomorrow.

Software development and coding

I started learning the Python language in 2014, as part of my Engineering Doctorate. I have developed object orientated models to evaluate cost of energy of wave energy farms (for my EngD) and offshore wind farms (at my previous employer, BVG Associates).

Programming is a growing passion of mine. During my career I have also developed Python modules for GIS analysis, integration with Microsoft Excel workbooks, and am currently developing a number of webapps using the Flask Python package.

I am also adept at Visual Basic (VBA) and have a working knowledge of SQL Server. I am also learning HTMl (so please excuse, or even better report, any issues you find with this website :D).

GIS

I started learning GIS towards the end of my EngD, in 2016, as I needed to create some maps to visualise the results from my research (more information can be found here.

Over the years I improved my GIS knowledge, leading and delivering several GIS projects at my previous employer BVG Associates (where I led GIS activities).

I continue to deliver and coordinate GIS work at my current employer, the Offshore Renewable Energy Catapult, where GIS remains one of my favourite pastimes.

You can read more about my GIS experience and projects here.
